The Problem

Beautiful product, broken experience

The client had exceptional product — but their old Shopify theme was generic, slow, and doing nothing to reflect the brand's positioning. Mobile conversion was under 1.2%. Cart abandonment was above 74%. Customers were leaving before they ever committed.

They needed a completely custom headless build that put the product first, eliminated friction, and made the checkout feel as premium as the product itself.

The Build

Every touchpoint considered

We built a headless Next.js storefront on top of a custom product API with Stripe for payments. The product detail experience includes animated reveals, a floating cart, size selection, and a wishlist — all without a single page reload.

◈
Headless Architecture
Fully custom front-end decoupled from the commerce layer — blazing fast, zero template constraints.
⬡
Slide-in Product Detail
Product pages open in a panel without losing the browsing context — keeps momentum high.
◉
Stripe Checkout
One-step checkout with saved cards, Apple Pay, and order confirmation in seconds.
▣
Wishlist & Filtering
Category filtering and wishlist persistence without account creation — zero barrier to saving.
