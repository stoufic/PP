The Problem

A consultancy that undersold itself

Nexus had a decade of results and a client roster that any firm would envy. Their website had none of that energy — it was a static WordPress site with a stock photo hero and a contact form that went to a generic inbox.

Prospects were doing due diligence, landing on the site, and moving on. The work was excellent. The presentation wasn't keeping up.

The Build

Every section doing work

We built a fully custom Next.js site with animated section transitions, a built-in contact flow, and a services matrix that made their offering immediately scannable. Every element was designed to reduce time-to-trust for cold visitors.

◉
Animated Hero
Gold sweep animations, radial glows, and a headline that commands attention on first load.
⬡
Tabbed Navigation
Single-page app feel — sections switch instantly without reload, keeping session momentum high.
▣
Built-In Contact Flow
Form captures name, email, company, and brief — qualified leads arrive pre-contextualized.
◈
Services Matrix
Six services displayed in a hoverable card grid — each with a gold underline reveal on interaction.
