The Problem

A great studio with a forgettable website

Forma Studio was closing deals through referrals alone — their website wasn't doing any of the selling. It was a dated WordPress theme with a PDF portfolio embedded in an iframe. Leads who found them cold were bouncing immediately.

They needed a site that worked as hard as their team — one that communicated their positioning instantly and made it easy for potential clients to understand the value of working with them.

The Build

Editorial design, engineered to convert

We built a fully custom Next.js site with a headless CMS (Sanity) so the team could publish new case studies without touching code. Case study reveals, cursor animations, and a showreel modal were all custom-built — no templates, no plugins.
